Ingredients:
3 eggs
3/4 cup Masa Harina (Corn flour)
1/2 Tbsp Baking powder (1tsp cream of tartar, 1/2tsp baking soda)
1/8th tsp chili powder
1/8th tsp pepper
1/8th tsp paprika
1/4 cup water
2tbspn oil of choice

Recipe:
Combine ingridients in bowl until smooth
Heat pan on medium heat until oil is hot 
Fry 4-6 minutes each side to desired level of crispness
Consume
Optional: Purchase salsa to consume with final product.
